Kylian Mbappé:

Kylian Mbappé Lottin is a French professional footballer who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the France national team. Mbappé began his senior career with Ligue 1 club Monaco, making his professional debut in 2015, aged 16.

Born: December 20, 1998 (age 22 years), 19th arrondissement of Paris, Paris, France

Height: 1.78 m

Weight: 73 kg

Nationality: French

Salary: 18 million EUR (2021)

Current teams: Paris Saint-Germain F.C. (#7 / Forward), France national football team (#10 / Forward)

Parents: Fayza Lamari, Wilfried Mbappé.
