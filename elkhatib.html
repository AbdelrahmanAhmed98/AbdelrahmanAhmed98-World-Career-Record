Mahmoud El Khatib:

Mahmoud El Khatib, popularly nicknamed Bibo, is an Egyptian retired footballer and current President of Al Ahly. He is widely regarded as one of the best players in African football history.

Born: October 30, 1954 (age 66 years), Qarqirah

Partner: Neven Farid Metwally

Height: 1.84 m (6 ft 0 in)

Current team: Al Ahly (president)

Children: Nada Mahmoud El Khatib

Awards: CAF Legend award, CAF Award for Best Ten players of the last 50 years
